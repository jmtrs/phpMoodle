Theme Options

Configurable Options

To access the configurable options for Theme Lambda go to Administration > Site Administration > Appearance > Themes > Theme-Lambda.

Page Background Settings

You can select the Page Background from a list of included background images or upload your own background image.

Included background images:

Country Road (default)
Bokeh Background
Blurred Background I
Blurred Background II
Blurred Background III
Cream Pixels (Pattern)
MochaGrunge (Pattern)
Skulls (Pattern)
SOS (Pattern)
Squairy Light (Pattern)
Subtle White Feathers (Pattern)
Tweed (Pattern)
Wet Snow (Pattern)

Note:
If you upload a custom image this will override any choice you make on the list of included backgrounds. If you wish to select one of these again, you have to delete your uploaded custom image.
